What is Animal Feed?

Animal feed refers to a specially formulated food product that is specifically designed to provide balanced nutrition and meet the dietary requirements of various animals. It is used to supplement or replace the natural diet of animals, ensuring that they receive the necessary nutrients for growth, development, reproduction, and overall health.


Our Products has enormous potential as a livestock animal feed due to: Its high content in proteins, essential amino acids, vitamins (vitamin A, vitamin B12, Beta Carotene), growth promoter intermediaries and minerals. Its ability to proliferate without inorganic nitrogen fertilization. Its high rate of growth in water without the need to displace existing crops or natural ecological systems.
