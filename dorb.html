At SunshineGreen, we take pride in our large-scale production capacity of 500 TPD crude oil, making us a potential supplier of De Oil Rice Bran across the country. De Oil Rice Bran is a highly versatile and valuable byproduct widely utilized in various industries. From animal feed manufacturing to fuel for boilers, as well as the production of silica gel, sodium silicate, and insulation bricks, De Oil Rice Bran offers a range of applications and benefits. Read on to explore the diverse uses of De Oil Rice Bran

Applications in Animal Feed Manufacturing

De Oil Rice Bran serves as a key ingredient in the formulation of animal feed, contributing to the nutritional well-being and performance of livestock and poultry. Its benefits include:


Cattle Feed:

De Oil Rice Bran is an excellent source of energy and essential nutrients for cattle, promoting healthy weight gain, milk production, and overall herd health.


Poultry Feed:

By incorporating De Oil Rice Bran into poultry feed, you enhance the protein content, improve egg production, enhance meat quality, and support the overall growth and vitality of poultry.

Usage as Fish Feed:

De Oil Rice Bran is an ideal component in fish feed formulations, benefiting aquatic species in several ways:


Nutrient-Rich:

De Oil Rice Bran provides a balanced mix of protein, carbohydrates, and essential minerals, promoting healthy growth, efficient feed conversion, and robust immune systems in fish.


Enhanced Flavor and Texture:

The inclusion of De Oil Rice Bran in fish feed can improve the taste, texture, and color of the harvested fish, enhancing its market value.

Industrial Applications:

Apart from its significance in animal feed and fuel production, De Oil Rice Bran finds applications in diverse industrial sectors:


Manufacturing of Silica Gel:

De Oil Rice Bran is a crucial raw material in the production of silica gel, a highly absorbent material used in various applications, including moisture control, packaging, and gas purification.


Sodium Silicate Production:

De Oil Rice Bran is utilized in the manufacturing process of sodium silicate, a versatile compound with applications in detergents, adhesives, water treatment, and construction materials.


Insulation Bricks:

De Oil Rice Bran contributes to the production of insulation bricks, providing thermal resistance and energy-efficient solutions for construction projects.
